Blackjack

Rules:

  • This would be single deck with continuous shuffling.
  • Each game has 5 players and a dealer
  • New game deals shuffled cards then deals card
  • Cards are dealt clockwise order, all player cards are face up. The dealer has the first card face up
  • Game checks if any hands hava blackjack
  • Player round commences. This goes clockwise
  • Your turn has two options: hit adds a card, stand finalises hand for the round
  • Dealer goes last
